group project that developed a hiking trail app, ‘HikeKnow’, that gathered weather and trail data on local hikes for the Island of Oahu and recommended daily hikes based off of information collected. front-end was built using the AngularJS framework, SASS and bulma library. also leveraged ngmap to show location of all trailheads on google maps. three separate API’s were called using request-promise and node-scheduler at various times during the day to keep all data relevant for each hike. deployed projects using Digital Ocean, Redis, Nginx, PM2 and Let’sEncrypt.
