hi, i’m michelle. i’m a newly trained fullstack web developer looking to transition into my first tech role. i completed a full-time four month coding bootcamp in hawaii learning to build and deploy full stack web application using HTML5, CSS, Javascript, React, AngularJS and Express. i also recently completed a five week python course on data analysis and visualization.

before moving into tech i worked in advertising fox six years - most recently as a production manager for a mural painting company - managing projects, communicating with clients and overseeing art jobs. in my spare time i enjoy going to community events in brooklyn, volunteering, all things drake, playing dice and am currently navigating my way around servers and learning linux. im open to internships, contract work, and both part and full-time roles.
